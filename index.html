User Guide

How to Connect Gmail:

1. Scroll to the top of the screen and look for an arrow icon

2. Click the arrow - this will reveal the "Connect Gmail" option

3. Click "Connect Gmail"

4. You'll be prompted to select from your Google accounts - choose the account you want to connect

5. Google will show you a warning about the permissions this tool will have. These permissions are limited by Google to ensure a secure connection and prevent spammy email sending apps that could hurt Google's reputation or enable misuse

6. Click "Advanced" in the warning dialog

7. Click "Continue to site"

8. You're now connected and ready to use the email generator with your Gmail account

These steps will safely connect your Gmail account while maintaining Google's security protections.
